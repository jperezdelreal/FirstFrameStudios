# Sprint 2 — Visual Quality Vision Brief

> Creative direction for Sprint 2. "Guilty Gear quality, not 1982."
> Minimum bar: Sega Mega Drive quality (Streets of Rage 2, Street Fighter II).

## The Bar
"Guilty Gear quality" means the game FEELS premium:
- Every hit has visual consequence (sparks, shake, hitstun)
- The stage reacts to violence (Ember escalation)
- The HUD communicates instantly (readable health, timer, combos)
- The AI reads like a player (approaches, combos, adapts)
- Characters have clear silhouettes and personality through animation

## Visual Identity Pillars
1. **Spectator clarity** — A viewer should understand who's winning at a glance
2. **Impact feedback** — Every hit looks and feels powerful
3. **Environmental storytelling** — The stage tells a story across rounds
4. **Character personality** — Kael and Rhena move differently, feel different
5. **UI as information** — HUD is clean, readable, and stylish

## Per-Domain Direction

### Camera
- Tight when fighters are close, zooms out when far
- Always keeps both fighters in middle 65% of screen
- Vertical compensation for jumps
- Reference: Guilty Gear Strive camera framing

### Character Sprites
- Detailed anatomy: face features, muscle contour, clothing detail
- Character-specific palettes: Kael (warm reds/oranges), Rhena (cool blues/purples)
- Animation personality: Kael = controlled power, Rhena = explosive speed

### Stage Art (Ember Grounds)
- Cracked obsidian floor with lava channels
- 3-round progression: dormant → warming → eruption
- Parallax depth layers for visual richness
- Ember particles and atmospheric haze

### HUD
- Modern fighting game style: gradient health bars, ghost damage, ember meter
- Round indicators, combo counter, announcer text
- Clean and readable at all times

### AI
- Aggressive, approaches the player, uses combos
- 3 difficulty levels for arcade mode progression
- Spacing intelligence — maintains fighting game distance
- Never stands still or blocks indefinitely

## What This Is NOT
- Not building Guilty Gear's engine or budget
- Not adding new characters or stages this sprint
- Not adding audio (Sprint 3)
- Not adding online multiplayer (future)
