## Player Attacks

| Attack | Startup | Active | Recovery | Total | Damage | Knockback | Hitbox (w×h) | Score per Hit |
|--------|---------|--------|----------|-------|--------|-----------|--------------|---------------|
| Punch  | ~0s (instant) | 0.15s | 0.15s | 0.30s | 10 | 150 | 40×40 | 10 |
| Kick   | ~0s (instant) | 0.25s | 0.25s | 0.50s | 15 | 250 | 60×30 | 20 |

### Notes
- **Startup** is effectively zero — hitbox appears on the same frame the attack starts.
- **Punch active window**: `attackCooldown` from 0.30 → 0.15 (hitbox alive while cooldown > 0.15).
- **Kick active window**: `attackCooldown` from 0.50 → 0.25 (hitbox alive while cooldown > 0.25).
- **Recovery** = cooldown remaining after hitbox disappears (can't input new attack until cooldown = 0).
- **Kick visual** (leg extend) plays while cooldown > 0.20; hitbox disappears at 0.25 → visual outlasts hitbox by 0.05s.
- **attackHitList** prevents the same enemy being hit twice by one attack.
- Knockback Y component is calculated as `(enemy.y - player.y) * 2`, adding depth-axis push.

### Player Hitbox Positions (relative to entity origin)
| Attack | X offset (facing right) | X offset (facing left) | Y offset |
|--------|------------------------|----------------------|----------|
| Punch  | x + 64 (entity right edge) | x - 40 | y + 20 |
| Kick   | x + 64 (entity right edge) | x - 60 | y + 30 |

## Enemy Attack

| Attack | Startup | Active | Recovery | Cooldown | Damage | Knockback | Hitbox (w×h) |
|--------|---------|--------|----------|----------|--------|-----------|--------------|
| Melee  | ~0s (instant) | ~1 frame (0.017s) | 0.50s (AI idle) | 1.50s | 5 | 100 | 35×35 |

### Notes
- **Active duration is extremely short**: AI sets `state='attack'` and `aiCooldown=0.5`, but on the very next frame AI sets `state='idle'` (because `aiCooldown > 0`). The hitbox is only checked once effectively.
- **attackCooldown** (1.5s) gates when the enemy can attack again.
- **aiCooldown** (0.5s) forces the enemy into idle after attacking.
- Knockback X = `enemy.facing * 100`. Knockback Y = `(player.y - enemy.y) * 1.5`.
- Enemy attacks require player within 80px distance AND 30px vertical proximity.

## QA Observations

1. **Enemy attack window is dangerously short** (~1 frame). Players may rarely get hit unless they walk directly into the hitbox on that exact frame. This explains the low difficulty feel.
2. **No combo system exists** — `player.comboCount` is not implemented. Attacks are independent with only cooldown gating.
3. **Kick visual outlasts hitbox** by 0.05s — the leg animation shows but does no damage during that window, which may confuse players.
4. **Knockback Y is position-relative**, not fixed — enemies above the player get knocked up, below get knocked down. Can cause unpredictable depth-axis scatter.
5. **Player has no attack startup** — hitbox appears instantly, making attacks feel like they have no wind-up. Combined with generous hitbox sizes, combat may feel too easy.
